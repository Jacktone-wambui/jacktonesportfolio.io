Frequently Asked Questions

Difference between UX and UI?

UI covers building the wireframes and prototypes and in my opinion most people have been employed as a result of building better UI. UX never ends and involves empathy and research. It is an iterative process and its the one that results to better UI.  

What do you love about UX?

I love listening to user stories and showing empathy.I believe UX has a high ROI on businesses.
